What does a 3 room apartment mean? (drei Zimmer)

A 3 room apartment means 1 living room and 2 bedrooms. You have a kitchen (separate or an &ldquo;open&rdquo; kitchen included in one of the rooms), a bathroom, a hallway. This is a 3 Zimmer Wohnung.
